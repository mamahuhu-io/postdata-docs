## GitHub Issues

If you have a bug to report or a feature request, please [open an issue](https://github.com/mamahuhu-io/postdata/issues/new/choose) on our GitHub repository. We will try to respond to your issue as soon as possible.

## GitHub Discussion

Our active community is comprised of more than 2,000 contributors. Asking your questions on our [GitHub Discussion](https://github.com/mamahuhu-io/postdata/discussions) board is a free and efficient way to ensure that you find solutions to your problems.

## Discord

Our [Discord server](https://postdata.cn/discord) is a good place to have real-time exchanges with members of the community. You can ask questions, get help, and discuss the future of Postdata. Join our Discord server to get started.

## Other platforms

We are also present on other platforms such as [X](https://postdata.cn/twitter), StackOverflow, and Reddit. You can follow us on these platforms to get the latest updates about Postdata.
